Royal Thorgan LLC

When some close friends of mine told me they were getting into real estate so they could gain positive cash flow and retire early, my curiosity was certainly piqued. When they told me the name of their prospective firm, I chuckled. But nonetheless, I was there to help -- pen in hand and ready to listen.

Thus was born Royal Thorgan -- an amalgamation of my friend Peter's middle name and my friend Andrew's strange fascination with footballer Eden Hazard's younger brother.

In order to match the esteem and regality evoked by such a name, the team needed a brand that would complement it. Immediately my mind jumped to the roaring lions of medieval kings and (more recently) similar to those emblazoned on the Chelsea football club jerseys. This allowed us to utilize well-established imagery of power and dominance by using the lion as a symbol, while doubling as a thematic link.

After several 'roaring lion' iterations we abandoned the more folksy style of yore, and opted for a modern approach. With extensive reference materials in tow, we took a shot at the roaring lion bust sans body -- which, in its essence, is what is seen here today.
